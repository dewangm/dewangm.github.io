Project Overview

Student who are studying in Seidenberg college are mostly unaware of the events and other social activites going on in the college, as there are no centralized social app/platforms which helps students be aware of events and form a social bond with other students, faculty.

Problem Statement:

The students do not feel they are part of the Seidenberg community because of poor social connections.

Goal:

To create an app to bring every Seidenberg student closer to create community.

Solution

The solution to the problem was to design an app with following features:

  • Allows user to access all events with easy registration.
  • Provides location of the events or lectures.
  • Connects to professors and tutors for guidance with easy appointments schedules.
  • Receive push notification of day-to-day events.

User Research

In order to design the app we conducted user research to know what essentials features are missing in current scenario of social platform for students.

After research, the key findings or missing features which most students wanted from new app where

  • Quick access to know about events and registrations.
  • Students did not know which professor is good for which course.
  • No Portal to book appointments with professors or academic advisors.
  • No information regarding existing clubs and volunteering events.

Based on the key finding, we brainstormed and synthesized the potential flow for the app.
